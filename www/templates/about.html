Purpose

The app gives you the tools needed to have complete control over your expences, regarding the most common consumables like:

Electricity Heating (like gas or water) Water Among others...

When you add to the stats each month, you get a brilliant overview of the expences and will be able to anticipate expences and spot use that exeeds the normal levels, and hence, take appropriate action.
